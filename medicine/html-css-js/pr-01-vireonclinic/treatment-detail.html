What to expect

During your consultation, we review your medical history, analyze facial proportions, and align on a conservative plan. Treatments are performed by physicians with advanced injector training.

  • Precise mapping and imaging for targeted placement.
  • Premium, biocompatible dermal fillers selected for your anatomy.
  • Follow-up evaluation within 2–3 weeks.

Ideal candidates

Patients seeking refined contour, volume restoration, or hydration without surgical intervention.

Recovery

Expect mild swelling or tenderness for 24–48 hours. We provide a personalized aftercare kit and check-ins.

Safety

All injectable treatments are administered by medical doctors in a fully equipped clinical environment.

Treatment pathway

How we plan your care.

1. Consultation

Medical review, facial assessment, and discussion of desired outcomes.

2. Treatment day

Numbing and precise injections with optional supportive therapies.

3. Follow-up

Review at 2–3 weeks to assess balance and determine if refinements are needed.

Results vary based on anatomy, product choice, and individual healing. A medical consultation is required.
