Terms of service

These terms outline participation expectations and responsibilities for NovaLearn Academy programs.

Enrollment and payment

Enrollment is confirmed after placement assessment and payment completion. Payment schedules are provided in writing before enrollment.

Attendance and participation

Learner progress depends on consistent attendance and completion of assigned practice. Missing sessions may affect outcomes.

Program changes

Program transfers are subject to availability and advisor approval. We will propose an alternative learning plan if necessary.

Outcome expectations

We provide clear guidance and coaching, but exam scores and proficiency gains vary by individual commitment and prior level.
